The firm

A practice built on trust, refined over generations.

Esquire Law was founded in 1962 by Sir Edmund Halpern KC and three colleagues who shared a conviction: that the law is a profession, not a transaction.

From a single set of chambers on Lincoln's Inn Fields, the firm has grown into one of the United Kingdom's most respected full-service practices — yet our founding principles remain unchanged. Every matter is led by a partner. Every client receives the same diligence, whether their case is a multi-jurisdictional M&A deal or a private family matter.

We are regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority and the Bar Standards Board. Our barristers hold rights of audience in England & Wales, and several are dual-qualified in New York.
