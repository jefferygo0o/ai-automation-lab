Commercial Litigation

The Firm

A chambers of
specialists, not generalists.

We were founded on a simple premise: that the most demanding clients deserve advisors who think before they speak, and who write before they bill.

Our barristers and solicitors are instructed individually and as a team on matters that often begin in private rooms and end in open court. We work alongside leading City firms, family offices, and overseas counsel.

Every brief is read by a senior member of chambers before it is accepted. Every fee is agreed in writing. Every piece of advice is reasoned, not asserted.
