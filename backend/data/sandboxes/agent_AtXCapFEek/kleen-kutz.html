Platform Features

Everything You Need to Run Your Shop

From booking to payments to client retention — Kleen Kutz gives you the tools to grow your barbershop business.

📅

Smart Online Booking

Clients book 24/7 through your custom portal. Automated reminders cut no-shows by 80%. Syncs with your calendar instantly.

💳

Integrated Payments

Accept credit cards, Apple Pay, and cash. Automatic tipping, invoicing, and daily sales reports. No more Square headaches.

👥

Client Management

Full client profiles with history, notes, photos, and preferences. Know exactly how each client likes their cut every time.

📊

Analytics & Insights

Real-time dashboard showing revenue, booking trends, popular services, and busiest hours. Make data-driven decisions.

📱

Client App

White-label mobile app for your shop. Clients book, reschedule, and browse services. Push notifications for promos.

📢

Marketing Suite

Email & SMS campaigns, loyalty rewards, referral programs, and automated follow-ups. Turn one-time clients into regulars.

🕒

Staff Management

Manage multiple barbers, commission splits, schedules, and time-off requests — all from one place.

🪒

Inventory Tracking

Track product inventory, supplies, and retail sales. Get low-stock alerts and automated reorder suggestions.

PCI Compliant

Enterprise-grade security. Your client data is encrypted, backed up, and fully compliant with payment industry standards.
